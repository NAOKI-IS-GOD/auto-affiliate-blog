Design & Fit

The Sennheiser MOMENTUM TW4 weighs 5.7g/片耳 per earbud for extended comfort. The Bose QC Earbuds II weighs 6.2g/片耳. Earphone comfort depends heavily on ear shape compatibility. Even the same person may prefer different tip sizes or materials. If possible, try them on in person before buying.

Sound Quality

The Sennheiser MOMENTUM TW4 supports aptX Adaptive/AAC/SBC codecs, including hi-res audio formats. The Bose QC Earbuds II supports AAC/SBC. Sound preference is subjective, but LDAC and aptX Adaptive transmit more audio data for better quality. Check if your streaming service supports hi-res audio before prioritizing codec support.

Active Noise Cancellation

Both models feature ANC for focus in noisy environments. The Sennheiser MOMENTUM TW4 delivers high-end ANC performance; the Bose QC Earbuds II also offers strong noise cancellation. If you mainly use earphones on trains, planes, or in cafes, ANC quality is a key criterion.

Battery Life

The Sennheiser MOMENTUM TW4 lasts 7.5時間+ケース per charge, totaling 最大43.5時間(ケース込み) with the case. The Bose QC Earbuds II: 6時間+ケース per charge, 最大30時間(ケース込み) total. For commuters and travelers, total case battery capacity matters most. If you always carry the case, 3-4 hours per earbud charge is often sufficient.
