Design & Case Size

The Apple Watch Series 10 uses a 46mm case weighing 41.7g. The Apple Watch Ultra 2 measures 49mm at 61.4g. For everyday wear, case size and weight directly affect comfort. Since you'll wear it all day, design preference matters more than on other gadgets.

Health & Fitness Features

The Apple Watch Series 10 offers 心拍/血中酸素/ECG/皮膚温度. The Apple Watch Ultra 2 includes 心拍/血中酸素/ECG/水中深度計. Both cover heart rate and blood oxygen as standard. The choice depends on whether you need serious athlete-grade tracking or everyday wellness monitoring.

Battery Life

The Apple Watch Series 10 lasts 18時間. The Apple Watch Ultra 2 achieves 60時間. Battery life is a critical differentiator for smartwatches. Whether you're OK with daily charging or need multi-day endurance will define the right choice.

Ecosystem & Smartphone Integration

The Apple Watch Series 10 is designed to integrate seamlessly with watchOS 11 devices. The Apple Watch Ultra 2 pairs best with watchOS 11. Smartwatch satisfaction is heavily influenced by smartphone pairing. Choosing a watch in your existing ecosystem almost always delivers the best experience.
